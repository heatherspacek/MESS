====== Overview ======
Created Monday 20 November 2023

The Melee Empirical Strategy Solver is an attempt to develop an "engine" resembling current computer chess engines that can determine a balance of  favourable or optimal strategies (and relevant counter-strategies) for Super Smash Bros Melee.
